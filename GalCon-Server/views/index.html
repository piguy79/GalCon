Story behind the app

We started this project with the aim of fulfilling a life long goal, to create a game. We each had our own exposure to games from a very young age, whether it be jumping platforms in Mario or wondering the halls of Wolfenstein. Given this was the major driving factor to choose a life in programming, we felt we owed it to ourselves to give it a shot.

In January 2013 we both faced long commutes to work and the lack of a creative outlet. It seemed like an ideal time to dedicate ourseleves to building comething. What followed was a few months of throwing ideas back and forth, prototyping and learning. Solar Smash started to take form from a number of these ideas. From the start our goal was first and foremost to create a game that we wanted to play, and now we hope you do too.

Frequently Asked Questions

How does the coin system work?

Every game you start will cost you one coin. When your coin count reaches 0, you will be unable to start a new game or accept a friends invite to play. Once you finish all in progress games, we will give you free coins. Alternativly you can buy more coins.

When will the IOS version be available?

We plan on releasing sometime in June 2014 to ios.

What is the best strategy for winning?

There are many stratagies people employ to win. Most people who pay attention to the cost benefit of capturing a planet do well. Make sure to take into account how much regeneration a planet has against how many ships it will take you to conquer that planet.

How does the xp system work?

XP (Experience points) is a system for determining a players rank. Moving through the ranks unlocks more levels in the game. Each planet conquered during a game grants the player XP. Winning a game will grant a large amount of XP to the victor.

Can I quit a game I know I am going to lose?

You can surrender using the resign button in the games options menu. This will grant the win to your opponent.

My opponent wont take a move, what are my options?

After a set timeout you will be prompted to "Claim Victory". Once you decide to claim, you will recieve the win.
